About BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator is a web-based health tool that helps users determine whether their body weight is healthy based on their height and weight. BMI is widely used as a quick method to classify individuals into different health categories.

How BMI is Calculated

BMI is calculated using the following formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (m)

Based on the calculated BMI value, the system determines the user's health category.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight – BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal Weight – BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight – BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese – BMI 30 or higher

Additional Features

In addition to calculating BMI, this system also provides personalized health suggestions. Based on the BMI category, the application recommends suitable diet plans and exercise routines to help users improve or maintain their health.

  • Healthy diet suggestions based on BMI category
  • Recommended exercises for fitness improvement
  • Weight management guidance

Purpose of the Project

The main goal of this project is to help users understand their body condition and guide them towards a healthier lifestyle by providing BMI calculation along with diet and exercise recommendations.
