Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find information about how to use the BMI Calculator.

How to Calculate BMI

Enter your age, gender, height, and weight in the BMI calculator and click the calculate button. The system will automatically show your BMI result and health category.

Health Suggestions

Based on your BMI, the system provides diet and exercise recommendations to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle.
